<Section title="The House Cup: Real-Time Battle">
The big feature driving fans wild is the **House Cup**. You're not playing alone; you join your house (Gryffindor, Slytherin, Ravenclaw, or Hufflepuff) and compete in **21-day cycles**. Every correct answer adds points to your faction in real-time.

Are you going to let the other houses take the lead? The season is underway, and every second counts to climb the global rankings.
</Section>

<Section title="Rewards Worthy of a Prefect">
It's not just about pride. The best players (MVPs) of the winning house receive:
*   **Exclusive Cards**: Unique digital art that only champions can collect.
*   **Official Recognition**: The names of the top wizards will appear on our YouTube channel.
*   **Visual Tribute**: The entire app changes its colors to honor the house that lifts the Cup.

With a perfect **5.0-star rating** and a community growing every minute, the verdict is clear: this is the game we’ve all been waiting for.
